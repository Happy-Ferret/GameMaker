            HOUSES

Houses illustrates how to make
buildings out of the blocks in
the HOUSES block set.  It also
illustrates  how  you can have
the  character    change    to
another character.

Use the arrow  keys  to  move.
Other  keys do various  things
for each of the characters.




Follow    the  green  path  to
change into another character.
To leave the  game,  press the
ESC key or enter the door with
the EXIT sign.
