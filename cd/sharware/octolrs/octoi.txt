Here's how to control Octolris
==============================
Left, right arrows:
----Walk those ways
Up arrow:
----Jump upwards
Up-left, up-right (Home,PgUp):
----Jump those ways
Down arrow:
----Duck, slide down
Slash (/):
----Climb up
==============================
The little blue hearts give
you a hit point and the vials
of slime enable you to climb.
